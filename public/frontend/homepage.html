Connect Farmers Directly with Customers

Empowering farmers with AI assistance and providing customers with fresh, local produce straight from the source.

Powerful Features

Our platform offers innovative tools to connect farmers with customers while providing AI-powered assistance.

Voice AI Assistant

Chat with our AI in your native language to get farming advice, crop management tips, and market insights.

Crop Analysis

Upload photos of your crops to get AI-powered analysis, disease detection, and growth recommendations.

Location-Based Discovery

Customers can find farmers near their location and purchase fresh produce directly from the source.

Secure Transactions

Safe and transparent payment system with order tracking and history for both parties.

Multi-language Support

Use the app in your native language with our comprehensive multi-language support system.

How It Works

Simple steps to connect farmers with customers and leverage AI assistance.

For Farmers

1

Create Your Profile

Register as a farmer, add your location, and set up your profile with details about your farm.

2

List Your Crops

Add your available crops with photos, prices, quantities, and harvest dates.

3

Get AI Assistance

Use voice chat or upload photos to get AI-powered advice on crop management.

4

Fulfill Orders

Receive orders from customers, arrange delivery or pickup, and get paid directly.

For Customers

1

Create Your Account

Register as a customer, set your location, and customize your preferences.

2

Discover Local Farmers

Browse farmers near you using the map or list view and filter by crop type.

3

Place Orders

Select crops from farmers, add them to your cart, and place your order securely.

4

Receive Fresh Produce

Get your order directly from the farmer through pickup or delivery options.
